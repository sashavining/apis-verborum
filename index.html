Rules

  • Guess in Latin
  • Words must be least 4 letters long.
  • Inflected forms will work, and each form will count as a separate word.
  • Only use letters from the game board.
  • You may use letters more than once.
  • Words must include the center letter.
  • "U" and "v" are counted as different letters
  • Come back tomorrow for a new puzzle! Your daily puzzle is unique to you, and refreshes daily.

Scoring

  • Four letter words are worth one point each
  • Words over four letters are worth one point per letter (e.g. a five letter word is worth five points)
  • A word that uses each letter at least once (pangram) will earn a bonus seven points.
